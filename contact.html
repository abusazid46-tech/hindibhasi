Frequently Asked Questions

How can I become a member?

You can become a member by filling the online registration form and paying the registration fee of ₹49. Your application will be reviewed by the admin and upon approval, you'll receive your membership certificate.

Is my donation tax-exempt?

Yes, all donations to Asom Hindi Bhasi Brahman Mahasabha are eligible for tax exemption under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act. You will receive a receipt for your donation.

How do I book a puja?

You can book pujas by contacting our office directly or through the puja booking section on our website. Our priests will contact you to discuss the details.

What are the office hours?

Our office is open Monday to Friday from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, and Saturday from 9:00 AM to 2:00 PM. We remain closed on Sundays and public holidays.
