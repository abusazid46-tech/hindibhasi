Asom Hindi Bhasi Brahman Mahasabha

Established in 1985, Asom Hindi Bhasi Brahman Mahasabha is a premier organization dedicated to the welfare and upliftment of Hindi-speaking Brahmin community in Assam. We work tirelessly to preserve our rich cultural heritage, promote education, and provide social support to community members in need.

Our Mission

To preserve and promote the cultural, social, and educational interests of the Hindi-speaking Brahmin community in Assam while contributing to the broader societal development.

Our Vision

To create a harmonious society where community members are empowered through education, cultural awareness, and economic opportunities while maintaining our traditional values.
