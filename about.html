About Our Organization

Preserving Culture, Serving Humanity Since 1980

Who We Are

Asom Hindi Bhasi Brahman Mahasabha is a registered non-profit organization dedicated to the welfare of the Brahmin community and society at large. Established in 1980, we have been working tirelessly to preserve and promote our rich cultural heritage, provide educational support, and organize religious and social activities.

Our organization serves as a platform for community members to come together, share knowledge, and work collectively for the betterment of society. We believe in the principles of dharma, service, and unity.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To preserve and promote Vedic culture, provide educational support to underprivileged students, organize religious ceremonies, and work for the social and economic upliftment of the community.

Our Vision

To create a harmonious society where traditional values and modern progress coexist, and every community member has access to education, healthcare, and opportunities for growth.

Our Journey

1980

Foundation

Asom Hindi Bhasi Brahman Mahasabha was established in Guwahati with 50 founding members.

1995

First Temple Renovation

Led the renovation of 5 ancient temples across Assam.

2005

Educational Trust

Established an educational trust providing scholarships to meritorious students.

2015

Digital Transformation

Launched digital initiatives to connect community members worldwide.

2024

Expansion

Now serving members across 27 districts with over 1500 active members.
